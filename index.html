Home

Holy Child School (English Medium recognized by the Directorate of Education , Uttar Pradesh, and affiliated to the Council for ISC Examination, New Delhi) is an unaided private institution owned by the society of sisters of charity. The management is vested in the hands of the sisters. It is a Christian minority Institution, holding a Minority Status Certificate. The school presently conduct classes form Kindergarten to class XII. The school follows the curriculum by the council for Indian School Certificate Examination (I.S.C) New Delhi.
